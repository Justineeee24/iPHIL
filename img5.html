BORACAY

TOURIST SPOTS

BORACAY BEACH

Boracay is famous for its white-sand beaches, vibrant nightlife, and water sports. White Beach, in particular, is renowned for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, making it a popular destination for tourists worldwide

FOODS

CHORI BURGER

A local street food favorite, it’s a grilled chorizo sausage patty served in a bun with a sweet and tangy sauce.

CALAMANSI MUFFINS

A delightful pastry made with the local citrus fruit, calamansi, offering a unique blend of sweet and tangy flavors.

SEAFOODS

Freshly caught seafood that you can have cooked to your liking at the D’Talipapa market, ensuring a personalized dining experience.

PLACES TO STAY

SHANGRI-LA'S BORACAY RESORT AND SPA

A luxury resort offering secluded villas, private beaches, and world-class amenities. It’s perfect for those seeking a luxurious getaway.

DISCOVERY SHORES BORACAY

Offers a range of accommodations from budget-friendly dormitories to private rooms with scenic views. The hotel features a restaurant, bar, and guided tours of the terraces.

THE LIND BORACAY

A beachfront resort offering modern rooms, an infinity pool, and a spa, known for its elegant design and top-notch service.
