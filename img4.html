CHOCOLATE HILLS ADVENTURE PARK

If you're up to bring your Chocolate Hills tour to a thrilling escapade, make sure to visit the Chocolate Hills Adventure Park (CHAP), where you can experience biking midair (Bike Zip) or surf zipline (The Wave Runner) overlooking the scenic view of the beautiful hills. It is an eco-tourism park located at Barangay Buenos Aires, Carmen. Apart from the Bike Zip and The Wave Runner, visitors can also enjoy the park's many activities and facilities, such as their Rope Courses, feed chickens and fishes, admire butterflies as well as the rich flora and fauna in "Ang Paru-Paro" Butterfly Garden, go horseback riding, and trek up and down their Eco-Hiking Trail, among others. Right before the long stairs leading to the viewing deck, visitors can check out the Serpentarium, where different species and sizes of snakes are aplenty.

THE PEACOCK GARDEN

A boutique resort featuring European-inspired decor, a wellness spa, and panoramic views of the sea and hills.

TARSIER SANCTUARY

Aside from the Chocolate Hills, Bohol is also known for its big-eyed mammals, the tarsiers. This is why Bohol is considered as one of the best places in the Philippines for a family vacation. These nocturnal creatures are one of the smallest primate in the world, about the size of a human hand. No Bohol experience is complete without seeing these small creatures in Tarsier Conservation tours.

FOODS

KINILAW

A Filipino ceviche made with fresh fish, vinegar, and spices. It’s a refreshing dish perfect for the tropical climate.

PEANUT KISSES

A popular local delicacy from Bohol, these are meringue cookies made with peanuts, resembling the Chocolate Hills.

KALAMAY

A sweet sticky rice delicacy cooked with coconut milk and sugar, often sold in coconut shells.

PLACES TO STAY

BOHOL BEACH CLUB

Located on Panglao Island, this resort offers beachfront accommodations, multiple dining options, and various water activities. It’s a great base for exploring Bohol.

AMORITA RESORT

A luxury resort on Alona Beach, offering modern amenities, infinity pools, and stunning views of the sea.

NATURA VISTA

Natura Vista is a tranquil boutique inn with tree-house vibes inside a fishing community of the San Pedro Public Beach in Dauis, Panglao Island, Bohol.
