VIGAN

TOURIST SPOTS

BANTAY CHURCH BELL TOWER

Nothing else can give you the best view of Vigan than the Bantay Church Bell Tower. The belfry is situated on a hill overlooking the Bantay district in northeast Vigan. You’ll need to climb a winding staircase to get to the top of the brick structure, where you’ll find an enormous bell hanging right in the middle of the tower. The watchtower served as a good vantage point for the natives of Ilocos to see approaching enemies during the Spanish reign and World War II. Today, people honor the memory of the tower by taking pictures at the foot of the hill, making it look as if they’re holding it dearly on the palm of their hands or forming a heart shape with their arms extended over the tower.

CALLE CRISOLOGO

A famous street in Vigan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It features Spanish colonial architecture and is a major tourist attraction. The cobblestone street is lined with well-preserved ancestral houses showcasing the beauty of the past.

VIGAN CATHEDRAL

Also called the Saint Paul Metropolitan Cathedral, the Vigan Cathedral is another important landmark in Ilocos Sur’s capital city and is also included in a Vigan City tour. It stands in a prominent spot just in front of Plaza Salcedo, which is why you might find many tourists having their pictures taken between these notable structures. The cathedral pays homage to Baroque architecture as seen in the ornate details outside and inside the church, although the cathedral has had its share of renovations too to make it sturdier.

FOODS

VIGAN LONGGANISA

A garlicky and slightly tangy local sausage that's a breakfast staple..

EMPANADA

A deep-fried pastry filled with Vigan longganisa, egg, and vegetables. It's a popular street food snack.

BAGNET

Deep-fried crispy pork belly, similar to lechon kawali. It is known for its crunchy skin and tender meat, often served with a dipping sauce.

PLACES TO STAY

HOTEL LUNA

Located near Calle Crisologo, this hotel combines modern luxury with historical charm. It features a museum showcasing Filipino art and artifacts.

CASA RICA HOTEL

A boutique hotel that offers a blend of traditional Filipino design and modern amenities, perfect for a comfortable stay in the heart of Vigan.

CIUDAD FERNANDINA HOTEL

Situated near Calle Crisologo, this hotel offers comfortable rooms with colonial-style architecture. It is an excellent base for exploring the city's historical sites.
